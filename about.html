Meet Tapestry Vertical Gardens

Design artistry meets horticultural precision.

Founded by Adam Shepherd — a graphic designer turned horticulturalist trained at The English Gardening School in Chelsea — Tapestry blends two decades of design with deep plant knowledge.

Adam personally oversees planting at the nursery and installation on site. Clients work directly with the designer, not a call centre, and every project carries his signature attention to detail.

A Philosophy Rooted in Plants

We begin with light, aspect, texture, and seasonal rhythm. Palettes are curated for compatibility as much as colour, so planting grows harmoniously over time. Herbs for fragrance, tropicals for lush interiors, pollinator-friendly species for biodiversity — each plan is bespoke.

How We Work

  1. Consultation — share your goals, constraints, and style.
  2. Design and Plant Selection — a tailored planting plan for your conditions.
  3. Nursery Growth — panels or structures are assembled and matured in Devon.
  4. Installation — rapid, low-disruption delivery with immediate impact.
  5. Care — maintenance to keep your garden thriving for years.
