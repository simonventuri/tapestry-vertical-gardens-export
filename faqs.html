Vertical Gardens — FAQs

How long does a vertical garden last?

With proper care and seasonal maintenance, your living wall can thrive for decades and mature beautifully over time.

Do vertical gardens require much maintenance?

Our hydroponic systems deliver efficient water and nutrients. We offer maintenance packages covering pruning, checks, and replanting where needed.

Can I have a vertical garden indoors?

Yes. Our clean, soil-free systems suit interiors. We can add lighting where natural light is limited.

How much does a vertical garden cost?

Costs depend on size, design, and plant selection. We provide a clear quotation after consultation.

How long does installation take?

Because we pre-grow in our Devon nursery, many projects install in days with immediate impact.

What if some plants fail?

As with any garden, occasional replacement is normal. Maintenance plans keep displays looking perfect.
